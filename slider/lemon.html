Accommodation Overview

The rooms are divide into Superior Business Rooms, Executive Garden Rooms, Executive Balcony Rooms and Executive Suite. The Superior Business Rooms come with free Wi-Fi and also Electronic safe and a Tea- Cofee maker. The Executive Balcony Room come with Bath tub and private sitting area. They also have Executive Garden Room with Mini Bar and Electronic safe and King and Twin bed options which makes your stay quite comfortable. Then they have Executive Suites with contemporary ambience and seperate living room and these suites are pool facing which makes them class apart. Also they give greater comfort and beautiful garden view.The other important facility provided by the hotel is the airport transfer for people staying in Executive Suite.
