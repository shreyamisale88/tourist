Hotel Description

Keys Hotel The Aures is one of the most elegant and well built hotel which provides great amenities and whether you are a business traveller or a leisure one you can stay at this hotel. It is located near Railway station road. If you are travelling with your family and visiting Aurangabad then certainly you should book a room in this hotel for a comfortable stay. The hotel is one of the best and has many in house facilities. It provides quick check-in facility so if you are coming from a long and tiring journey then this is the best place to head and also the rooms are decent with great space and cooling. The cafeteria serves varieties of food which are either continental, Punjabi and also variety of foods is served here. Along with other facilities there are rooms for differently abled and in-room dining with tea and cofee maker and free bottled water. The hotel provides currency exchange and vending machine to the guests so that they could easily exchange currencies. The front office staff is very helpful and the amenities are great. So, altogether its a great place to stay.

Accommodation Overview

The rooms are divided into Executive rooms, Business rooms and Club rooms. The Executive rooms which have ergonomically designed work desk and it helps you to get going efficiently. The bathroom is glass panelled and bathroom has a walk-in-shower which helps you to relax from a long day at your conference or business meeting. The other kind of room is the business room which has the same set of bathroom and it is well equipped to provide you all the leisure at your room. The other kind of room is the club room which is high on aesthetics and appeal and if you book this room you will be able to get the complimentary air-port drop as well. Each of the room is pre-loaded with premium benefits and give you a high order experience.
