Hotel Description

Hotel Raviraj is one of the most elegant hotels in Aurangabad by Panchwati Group. The famous hotel has great amenities which include 42 rooms, family restaurant and kids lay zone along with conference hall and gym. The hotel fulfills all the basic requirements with breakfast as per order and good wi-fi speed which makes it an ideal business as well family hotel.
