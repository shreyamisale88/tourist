Hotel Description

Hotel Preetam Aurangabad is one of the budget accommodations for travellers coming to Aurangabad. It is ideally situated within one minute walking distance from the railway station at Pdampani colony. The hotel is a budgeted accommodation which makes it ideal for backpackers and budget travellers. The facilities are good and it has twenty four rooms with AC and all the rooms are renovated recently. If you are concerned about the safety then you will be glad to know that the whole area is under CCTV Surveillance. The maintenance of the lodge is very good and it is managed by diligent staff who provides 24 hours room service with 24*7 travel desk. Hotel provides generator facility for power back up and laundry services. There are two nearby restaurant from where one can order sumptuous foods. It is easy to stay here as one can use any credit card because the hotel accepts all major credit cards. So, one can see that the hotel provides all the facility to its guests which makes it one of the most visited hotel by the guests.
