Hotel Description

Welcome Hotel Rama International is one of the famous hotels by ITC Group and is the most elegant and distinguished hotels which is a five star property. It is located at Chikalthana, Aurangabad and has 132 rooms including deluxe suites which suits the needs of the travellers to the town who are in search of some peace at Sai Baba Mandir or they are looking to see the world renowned Ajanta and Ellora caves. It is also suitable for the high end business class persons who come here to attend meeting or conferences as their are many industries in the vicinity. The hotel is known for providing great amenities such as Wi-Fi in every room along with clean and healthy environment. One can avail the facility of swimming pool to refresh themselves and enjoy swimming at any hours. The multi-cuisine restaurant provides a great deal of options for the foodies to enjoy various kinds of delicious foods. Also, a sports bar by the name of Pit-Stop is here which gives one opportunity to enjoy exotic drinks. In case if a person is travelling by their own vehicle then they can get the parking facility at the hotel. Also, the hotel is known for giving great facility to the travellers as there are many people coming to visit Aurangabad. So, if you are one of them then certainly you should choose this hotel for your stay.
