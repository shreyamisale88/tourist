Hotel Description

The Ambassador Ajanta is a great hotel with an old world charm with new look. The hotel is located at Jalna road opposite high court. The hotel premises is very big with a beautiful garden. The rooms are neat and clean and it has a grand entrance along with the reception. It has a jogging track along with a zodiac garden. Also, they have rooms facing pool which gives a great view. It is a good hotel for budget travellers and is close proximity to the city centre makes it easily accessible. The restaurant here serves good variety of breakfast and its huge quiet campus add to its luxury. It is also one of the well maintained hotel with best stay options. The rooms are good and facilities are great. It also provides free parking and free wi-fi in the room. So, all kinds of amenities are provided at this hotel giving one a comfortable stay.
